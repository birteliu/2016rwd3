About Lowden Workshop

George Lowden Guitars Hand made in Ireland

We began making guitars in earnest in 1974 and our workshops have developed over the intervening years to our present team of 13 skilled craftsmen (including two of George's sons Aaron and Daniel) building Lowden guitars by hand.
